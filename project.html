Project Work

Week 1 – Community Survey & Awareness (30-06-25 to 05-07-25)

  • Visited Sachivalayam in NTR Nagar to take permission for conducting the nutrition survey.
  • Conducted the survey in different streets of NTR Nagar, Nellore district.
  • Collected detailed information on food habits and nutrition awareness.
  • Documented difficulties in following a proper diet due to financial and lifestyle limitations.
  • Started creating awareness about healthy food habits and balanced diets.
  • Visited houses to explain practical dietary changes families can adopt.

Week 2 – Data Entry & Analysis of Survey (07-07-25 to 12-07-25)

  • Planned our awareness speech and structured the survey and talk.
  • Conducted an awareness programme and encouraged sharing of experiences.
  • Explained how to maintain a nutritious diet using daily examples.
  • Spoke about the importance of a balanced diet as a basic need.
  • Continued the survey and noted problems faced by citizens.
  • Visited more houses and listened to families’ challenges with empathy.

Week 3 – Planning the NutriGuide Website (14-07-25 to 19-07-25)

  • Measured BMI of people using height and weight.
  • Continued BMI measurements and practiced patience.
  • Calculated BMI values and organized the data.
  • Advised people in early obesity stages on diet and lifestyle changes.
  • Educated overweight individuals on diet and exercise.

Week 4 – Designing and Coding the Website (21-07-25 to 26-07-25)

  • Explained healthy food habits and balanced diets during house visits.
  • Conducted awareness sessions using affordable local foods.
  • Promoted nutritious food and exercise like yoga.
  • Distributed fruits to promote simple and nutritious eating.

Week 5 – Testing, Feedback & Refinement (28-07-25 to 02-08-25)

  • Visited the school and took permission for nutrition awareness activities.
  • Conducted a programme explaining regular meals and healthy snacks.
  • Surveyed students about food habits and identified health issues.
  • Explained how food and exercise support a healthy life.
  • Motivated younger students to follow healthy habits.
  • Continued awareness activities and talked with students individually.

Week 6 – Awareness Programme & Final Documentation (04-08-25 to 09-08-25)

  • Festival of Bakrid – respected community occasions and paused survey.
  • Awareness session for diabetic individuals about avoiding sugary foods.
  • Shared detailed diet control tips for diabetic people.
  • Collected feedback from the community about our awareness programmes.
  • Continued informing people about healthy food habits and precautions.
  • Final day of our project. Took photographs and completed documentation.
