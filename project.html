Project Work

Week 1 – Community Survey & Awareness (30-06-25 to 05-07-25)

  • Day 1: Visited Sachivalayam in NTR Nagar to take permission for conducting the nutrition survey.
  • Day 2: Conducted the survey in different streets of NTR Nagar, Nellore district, interacting with community members.
  • Day 3: Collected detailed information on daily food habits, preferences and nutrition awareness of the people.
  • Day 4: Documented difficulties people face in following a proper diet due to financial and lifestyle limitations.
  • Day 5: Started creating awareness about healthy food habits and balanced diets for all age groups.
  • Day 6: Visited houses to spread awareness and explain practical dietary changes families can adopt.

Week 2 – Data Entry & Analysis of Survey (07-07-25 to 12-07-25)

  • Day 1: Planned our awareness speech and structured the survey and talk for the community.
  • Day 2: Conducted an awareness programme and encouraged people to share their experiences.
  • Day 3: Explained how to maintain a nutritious diet using simple daily examples.
  • Day 4: Spoke about the importance of a balanced diet as a basic need.
  • Day 5: Continued the survey and noted problems faced in following healthy food habits.
  • Day 6: Visited more houses and listened to families’ challenges with empathy and patience.

Week 3 – Planning the NutriGuide Website (14-07-25 to 19-07-25)

  • Day 1: Measured BMI of people using height and weight to share health knowledge practically.
  • Day 2: Continued BMI measurements and practiced patience during data collection.
  • Day 3: Calculated BMI values and organized the collected data.
  • Day 4: Advised people in early obesity stages on diet and lifestyle changes.
  • Day 5: Educated overweight individuals on diet and exercise to raise awareness.

Week 4 – Designing and Coding the Website (21-07-25 to 26-07-25)

  • Day 1: Explained healthy food habits and balanced diets during house visits.
  • Day 2: Conducted awareness sessions using affordable local foods.
  • Day 3: Promoted nutritious food and exercise like yoga for health.
  • Day 4: Distributed fruits to promote simple and nutritious eating.

Week 5 – Testing, Feedback & Refinement (28-07-25 to 02-08-25)

  • Day 1: Took permission from the school to conduct nutrition awareness activities.
  • Day 2: Conducted a programme explaining the importance of regular meals and healthy snacks.
  • Day 3: Surveyed students about food habits and identified health issues.
  • Day 4: Explained how food and exercise together support a healthy life.
  • Day 5: Motivated younger students to follow healthy habits from childhood.
  • Day 6: Continued awareness activities and talked with students individually.

Week 6 – Awareness Programme & Final Documentation (04-08-25 to 09-08-25)

  • Day 1: Festival of Bakrid – respected community occasions and paused survey.
  • Day 2: Awareness session for diabetic individuals about avoiding sugary foods.
  • Day 3: Shared detailed diet control tips for diabetic people.
  • Day 4: Collected feedback from the community about our awareness programmes.
  • Day 5: Continued informing people about healthy food habits and precautions.
  • Day 6: Final day of our project. Took photographs of major activities and completed documentation for presentation.
