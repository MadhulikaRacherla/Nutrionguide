Nutrition and Sustainability

Healthy food habits go hand-in-hand with a healthy planet. Our mission includes promoting sustainable agriculture and supporting global reforestation efforts.

Reforestation supports climate action, local communities, and helps reduce deforestation — all of which impact food systems and nutrition.
